# ISSUE-0058: Revenue Reporting

- Title: Revenue Reporting
- Epic: EPIC-007 Monetization
- Priority: P3
- MVP: No
- Milestone: M6 - Monetization
- Story ID: MN-007

## User Story

As an operator, I want revenue reporting so monetization can be evaluated.

## Description

Create reporting for early monetization experiments across referrals, partner venues, sponsored placements, and offers.

## Acceptance Criteria

- Reports show revenue or attributed value by campaign or partner.
- Reports distinguish experimental, estimated, and confirmed revenue.
- Refunds, reversals, or invalid attribution can be represented.
- Data access is restricted to authorized operators.

## Technical Notes

- Build on attribution and business analytics events.
- Avoid financial dashboards before real monetization data exists.
