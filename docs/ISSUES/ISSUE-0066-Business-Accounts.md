# ISSUE-0066: Business Accounts

- Title: Business Accounts
- Epic: EPIC-009 Business Tools
- Priority: P3
- MVP: No
- Milestone: M6 - Monetization
- Story ID: BT-001

## User Story

As a venue owner, I want a business account so I can manage my presence.

## Description

Create business account foundations for venues that want to participate in planning activity.

## Acceptance Criteria

- A business account can be created or claimed through a controlled workflow.
- Business users have roles or permissions separate from consumer users.
- Business accounts cannot edit user planning data.
- Verification expectations are documented.

## Technical Notes

- Build on product auth rather than Differance Labs auth.
- Keep ownership and venue claim data auditable.
