# LATER

Build after traction.

LATER contains strategic expansion work that should wait until the planning loop, repeat social behavior, and reliability foundation are validated.

## Strategic Priorities

- Business platform.
- Monetization.
- Agent ecosystem.
- APIs.
- Third-party integrations.
- Relationship graph.
- Premium planning.
- Sponsored recommendations.

## Issue-Ready Outcomes

- Venue partners can measure planning influence.
- Sponsored or partner recommendations are clearly labeled.
- Premium planning concepts are validated before billing work begins.
- External assistants can draft plans through approved APIs.
- Relationship graph signals improve planning outcomes without hiding assumptions.

## Entry Criteria

- Core planning retention is proven.
- Recommendation behavior is measurable.
- Business demand is validated through interviews or pilots.
- Provider secrets and commercial workflows are ready for server-side handling.
- Agent permissions, audit trails, and user approvals are designed before external access.
