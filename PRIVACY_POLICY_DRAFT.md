# Privacy Policy for Things Nearby

Effective date: Replace before publishing

Things Nearby helps users find food and activity ideas nearby.

## Information we collect
The app may request foreground location permission when you tap “Use my location.” Location is used to help open nearby map searches. The app does not collect background location.

This MVP does not require an account and does not sell personal data.

## Third-party services
When you tap “Find this near me,” the app opens Google Maps or a web browser search. Those services may process your search and location according to their own policies.

## Data retention
This MVP does not maintain a server-side account database.
