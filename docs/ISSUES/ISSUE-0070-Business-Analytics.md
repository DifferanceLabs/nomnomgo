# ISSUE-0070: Business Analytics

- Title: Business Analytics
- Epic: EPIC-009 Business Tools
- Priority: P3
- MVP: No
- Milestone: M6 - Monetization
- Story ID: BT-005

## User Story

As a venue owner, I want analytics so I know what is working.

## Description

Create business analytics events and reports that help venues understand campaign and offer performance.

## Acceptance Criteria

- Business analytics track impressions, interactions, referrals, and campaign outcomes.
- Reports are scoped to authorized business accounts.
- User privacy and plan privacy are preserved.
- Analytics definitions align with product analytics taxonomy.

## Technical Notes

- Build on core analytics rather than creating a disconnected event system.
- Keep early reports simple and explainable.

## Future Considerations
