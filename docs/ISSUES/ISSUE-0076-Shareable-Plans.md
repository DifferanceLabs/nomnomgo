# ISSUE-0076: Shareable Plans

- Title: Shareable Plans
- Epic: EPIC-010 Growth
- Priority: P0
- MVP: Yes
- Milestone: M1 - Local MVP
- Story ID: GR-004

## User Story

As a participant, I want shareable plans so I can send the outcome to others.

## Description

Make open and locked plans shareable through stable links and concise share copy.

## Acceptance Criteria

- Users can share open plans for participation.
- Users can share locked plans as final outcomes.
- Share permissions are respected for private plans.
- Shared links route through the canonical plan path.

## Technical Notes

- Coordinate with share sheet and deep link work.
- Keep share copy short and platform-neutral.
