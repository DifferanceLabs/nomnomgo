# NomNomGo Vision

## Purpose

NomNomGo helps people come together.

People increasingly live through fragmented group chats, social feeds, calendars, recommendation engines, and AI assistants. The work of planning real-world activity is spread across too many tools: someone suggests an idea in chat, someone searches maps, someone checks a calendar, someone remembers preferences, and everyone waits for a decision.

NomNomGo becomes the shared planning layer that helps friends decide what to do, where to go, and when to meet.

## Product Thesis

The first wedge is simple: small groups can plan dinner, activities, events, or mixed outings without leaving the app.

The larger opportunity is the coordination layer around real-world relationships. If NomNomGo understands groups, shared preferences, planning history, constraints, and outcomes, it can make future plans easier for people, more useful for businesses, and more accessible to AI assistants.

## Long-Term Direction

NomNomGo becomes the social planning network.

Eventually, it becomes a relationship-aware platform that allows people, businesses, and AI agents to coordinate real-world activity.

## Who It Serves

- Friends deciding where to eat or what to do.
- Couples, families, roommates, and recurring small groups.
- Visitors exploring unfamiliar areas together.
- Venues that want to participate in planning moments before decisions are made.
- AI assistants that need a trusted planning surface for human approval.

## Product Promise

NomNomGo turns "What should we do?" into a shared, visible, low-friction decision.

The app should make the next step obvious:

- Start a plan.
- Invite the group.
- Suggest or receive options.
- Vote or converge.
- Lock the plan.
- Remember what happened.

## Strategic Bets

1. Group intent is more valuable than individual browsing intent.
2. Planning is a better social primitive than passive posting.
3. Recommendations improve when they understand the people deciding together.
4. Businesses benefit most when they appear before the decision is final.
5. AI assistants need transparent coordination tools where humans remain in control.

## What NomNomGo Is Not

- Not a generic social feed.
- Not a restaurant search clone.
- Not a permanent dependency on Differance Labs authentication.
- Not an ad-first recommendation engine.
- Not an AI system that decides for people.

## 12-Month Outcome

Within 12 months, NomNomGo should have:

- A reliable planning MVP used by active groups.
- Repeat usage from groups that plan more than once.
- Basic social primitives around friends, groups, and saved places.
- Observable planning, recommendation, and retention metrics.
- A foundation for venue partnerships and monetization pilots.

## 24-Month Outcome

Within 24 months, NomNomGo should have:

- A mature social planning loop.
- Group-aware recommendations that outperform manual search for core use cases.
- First revenue from venue or premium planning models.
- Business tools that let venues participate without controlling the user experience.
- Early agent integrations that can create, update, and coordinate plans with human approval.
