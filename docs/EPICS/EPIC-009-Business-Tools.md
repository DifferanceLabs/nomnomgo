# EPIC-009: Business Tools

Status: Proposed

Primary phase: Phase 4, Business Platform

## Mission

Help venues participate in planning moments.

## User Outcome

Businesses can present accurate, useful, and measurable offers to groups without taking over the planning experience.

## Stories

| ID | Story | Priority | Acceptance Signal |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| BT-001 | As a venue owner, I want a business account so I can manage my presence. | Later | Businesses can claim or create an account with verification rules. |
| BT-002 | As a venue owner, I want a dashboard so I can see planning activity. | Later | Dashboards show relevant impressions, clicks, referrals, and locked plans. |
| BT-003 | As a venue owner, I want offer management so I can create group-relevant promotions. | Later | Offers can be created, scheduled, paused, and measured. |
| BT-004 | As a venue owner, I want event promotion so I can reach groups considering activities. | Later | Businesses can promote events through labeled, relevant placements. |
| BT-005 | As a venue owner, I want analytics so I know what is working. | Later | Reports show campaign performance and conversion signals. |
| BT-006 | As a marketer, I want campaign tracking so spend can be attributed. | Later | Campaigns have identifiers, budgets or limits, and performance reports. |
| BT-007 | As a user, I want accurate venue data so business participation improves planning. | Later | Business-managed fields are verified or clearly sourced. |

## Technical Backlog

- Business account model.
- Venue claim and verification flow.
- Business dashboard.
- Offer management model.
- Campaign tracking model.
- Analytics pipeline for business events.
- Permission model for business users.
- Moderation workflow for business content.

## Definition Of Done

- Business tools create measurable value without hiding commercial influence.
- Users can distinguish organic, partner, and sponsored venue information.
- Business accounts cannot edit user planning data.

## Metrics

- Claimed venue count.
- Offer creation count.
- Offer redemption or referral rate.
- Business dashboard retention.
- Revenue influenced by business tools.
