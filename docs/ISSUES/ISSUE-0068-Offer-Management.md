# ISSUE-0068: Offer Management

- Title: Offer Management
- Epic: EPIC-009 Business Tools
- Priority: P3
- MVP: No
- Milestone: M6 - Monetization
- Story ID: BT-003

## User Story

As a venue owner, I want offer management so I can create group-relevant promotions.

## Description

Allow business users to create, schedule, pause, and measure simple offers for planning contexts.

## Acceptance Criteria

- Business users can create an offer with title, terms, schedule, and venue.
- Offers can be paused or expired.
- Offer visibility is governed by relevance and labeling rules.
- Offer changes are audited.

## Technical Notes

- Keep offers separate from sponsored ranking.
- Avoid payment, redemption, or POS integration in first version.

## Dependencies

- ISSUE-0053
- ISSUE-0066

## Future Considerations

- Redemption codes and booking integrations can follow partner pilots.
