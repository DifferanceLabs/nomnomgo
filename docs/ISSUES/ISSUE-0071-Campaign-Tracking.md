# ISSUE-0071: Campaign Tracking

- Title: Campaign Tracking
- Epic: EPIC-009 Business Tools
- Priority: P3
- MVP: No
- Milestone: M6 - Monetization
- Story ID: BT-006

## User Story

As a marketer, I want campaign tracking so spend can be attributed.

## Description

Add campaign identifiers, status, limits, and reporting hooks for business promotions.

## Acceptance Criteria

- Campaigns can group offers or promoted events.
- Campaigns have status and optional budget or exposure limits.
- Campaign interactions are tracked consistently.
- Campaign reporting is available to authorized business users.

## Technical Notes

- Avoid spend automation until billing exists.
- Keep campaign IDs stable for attribution.
