# Google Play Store Listing Draft

## App name
Things Nearby

## Short description
Personal food and activity ideas for right now.

## Full description
Things Nearby helps you decide what to eat or do when you do not want to overthink it. Pick your mood, choose the weather feel, and get a ranked list of simple nearby ideas: breakfast, coffee walks, parks, bowling, dinner ideas, mini golf, and more.

The app is designed for quick everyday decisions, family outings, low-effort date nights, and last-minute plans.

Features:
- Mood-based suggestions
- Food and activity ideas
- Kid-friendly, cheap, date-night, and rainy-day filters
- Optional location-assisted map search
- No account required
- No background location tracking

## Category
Lifestyle or Travel & Local

## Content rating notes
No mature content, no user-generated content, no commerce inside the app.

## Data safety draft
Data collected: Location, only when user grants foreground permission.
Data shared: No developer-operated sharing in MVP. Google Maps/browser may process searches when opened by the user.
Security practices: No account required. No server-side user profile in MVP.
